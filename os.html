There is a company called JP Aerospace which is actively working on Airship To Orbit program which aims to lower the cost of lifting mass to orbit from today's 1000$/kg to 0.31$/kg. One issue of this system is that the journey to orbit will take nine days, but it can easily be resolved by deploying multiple ATO systems at once. The other issue is that this system may not reach orbital speed at all. Nobody has tried accelerating 2km-long airships to orbital speed at the edge of space before, so it is a bet whether this will work or not, but I think it is a bet worth taking.
